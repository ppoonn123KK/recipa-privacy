Classic Beef Burger

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 10 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) ground beef (80% lean / 20% fat recommended)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Toppings: Lettuce, sliced tomato, red onion, pickles, and cheese (optional)
  • Sauce: Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Shape the Patties: Divide the beef into 4 equal portions. Gently shape them into rounds about 1 inch thick. Do not overwork the meat.
  2. Season: Generously season both sides of each patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the Pan: Heat oil in a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook: Place patties in the pan. Cook for 3â€“4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer to your preferred doneness. If using cheese, add a slice in the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast the Buns: While the meat rests, lightly toast the buns until golden brown.
  6. Assemble: Place the patty on the bottom bun. Add your favorite toppings and spread sauce on the top bun. Close and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade burger!
